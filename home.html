About

Hi there! My name is Alex Suglio, I'm a web developer with experience in marketing, graphic design, and multimedia production. I attended the University of Florida where I graduated with a degree in Marketing and also studied film and copywriting independently. In college I was the cartoonist for our student paper, The Alligator, the largest student run paper in the country, and wrote freelance articles for CollegeHumor.com.

After college my interests in Marketing and TV landed me a job in San Diego, writing and producing Direct Response Television Commercials for Medicus Golf. Several of my spots have been featured, nationally, on NBC Golf Channel and various other cable networks. I also worked in digital marketing, creating webcopy and webmercials, which introduced me to a number of programmers in software development. Our interactions got me more interested in programming and inspired me to learn JavaScript and HTML.

My passion is design and functionality. I believe UX/UI is ultimately what keeps a client on your website or application. If you've lost the user's interest, you've lost a sale. Through my studies, I've discovered how the fundamental principles of marketing apply to web development. I'm excited to take what I've learned from the classroom, soundstage, and editing bay to create an experience for you which is truly something different. Stay tuned.
