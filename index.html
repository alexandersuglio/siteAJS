About

Hi there! My name is Alex Suglio, I'm a web developer with experience in media production, mainly commericals. I grew up in South Florida, and attended the University of Florida where I majored in Marketing but studied film and media production on my own. After college my interests in Marketing and TV landed me a job in San Diego, writing and producing Direct Response Television Commercials for Medicus Golf. Serveral of my spots have been featured on NBC Golf Channel and various other cable stations.

In college I was the cartoonist for our student paper, The Alligator, the largest student run paper in the country, and even wrote a few articles for CollegeHumor.com.
